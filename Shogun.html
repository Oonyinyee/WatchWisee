Top TV Show of 2024: [Shogun]

The 2024 adaptation of Shogun brings James Clavell’s epic novel back to the screen with striking visual splendour and a deeper dive into the cultural complexities of feudal Japan. With a more nuanced approach than its 1980 predecessor, this version emphasizes the clash of cultures as English navigator John Blackthorne (played with intensity and restraint) is thrown into a world of samurai honour, rigid tradition, and political intrigue. The show boasts stunning cinematography and meticulous set design, making each scene feel like a painted tapestry of Japan's Edo period.

While at times the pacing may feel deliberate, it allows for richer character development, particularly between Blackthorne and the influential samurai Lord Toranaga, whose portrayal here is layered and magnetic. Shogun is a treat for viewers looking for a character-driven, visually immersive experience that thoughtfully explores East meets West dynamics. It's an intense journey that rewards patience with drama and historical depth.

The show was created by Rachel Kondo and Justin Marks

Shogun premiered exclusively on Disney+ on the 27th of February, 2024

Rating:8.6/10
