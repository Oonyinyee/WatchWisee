Mega-hit:[Deadpool & Wolverine]

Deadpool and Wolverine delivers exactly what fans have been hoping for: relentless action, sharp humor, and the perfect clash of personalities. Ryan Reynolds' Deadpool brings his signature sarcasm, breaking every fourth wall in sight, while Hugh Jackman’s Wolverine returns with all the gruff, gritty charm that made him an icon. The film’s R-rated action sequences are wildly entertaining, balancing brutal combat with laugh-out-loud moments. Though the plot may not be groundbreaking, the chemistry between Deadpool and Wolverine and the sheer fun of watching them team up (and clash) make it a must-see for superhero fans looking for something edgier and more unfiltered

The film was directed by Shawn Levy, he also directed the Night at the Museum movies and Free Guy which stars Ryan Reynolds

The film was released in cinemas on the 25th of July, 2024

Rating:7.8/10
